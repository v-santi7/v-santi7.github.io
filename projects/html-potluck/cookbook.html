Crispy Bagel Bites

These tortilla pinwheels are quick and easy to make with just a few basic ingredients. Simply roll, cut, and crisp them up in a skillet with butter until golden brown. Serve as a yummy snack or quick breakfast! My kids especially love this simple recipe.

Prep Time: 5 mins
Cook Time: 5 min

Course: Breakfast, snack

Keyword: Easy snack, Fun food, Quick breakfast

Serving: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 2-3 tbsp cream cheese
  • Everything Bagel seasoning (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Use a butter knife to evenly spread the cream cheese all over your tortilla and then sprinkle with seasoning

  2. Tightly roll the tortilla up into a log shape and then cut into bite-size pieces. I usually get about 9 pinwheels out of one large tortilla.

  3. Melt 1 tbsp of butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add your pinwheels and stir them into the butter, evenly coating all sides. Cook, flipping them often, until golden brown and crispy.

  4. Allow them to rest for about 5 minutes before enjoying. They are much better once the cream cheese has hardened a bit!
